The Department is committed to providing funding packages that match or beat those of other MA programs. Students in this graduate program will: Take advanced Political Science courses in a range of specialized subjects Have the opportunity of applying to participate in a paid internship Write an advanced research paper Become a paid teaching assistant Have the opportunity of securing a research position Be eligible for exceptional entrance scholarships M.A. with Internship In this academically rigorous program you will have the chance to earn an MA degree through coursework and a paid internship The internship option is designed to combine first class academic work with practical employment experience that will give you an advantage in the workplace. Students in this graduate program will: Have the opportunity of participating in a paid internship Secure exceptional real life work experience Become a paid teaching assistant Have the opportunity of securing a research position Be eligible for exceptional entrance scholarships Program Specific Admission Requirements Deadline for Fall: June 1 Deadline for Winter: September 1 Minimum TOEFL: IBT-100 Minimum IELTS: 7.0 Minimum Pearson: 68 Minimum MELAB: 84 Minimum CAEL: 70 Additional Requirements Two academic Referee Assessment forms Current Resume/CV Statement of Purpose (limit to one page) Writing sample which should be program related (10 pages or less) General admission requirements for all Graduate Programs Scholarships and Funding Learn More Request More Information Graduate Academic Program and Course DescriptionsVisit Political ScienceApply to this Program share tweet save Grad News COVID-19 updates from the Faculty of Graduate Studies Postponed: Three Minute Thesis competition 2020 Graduate studies features in the DailyNews Fine Arts Grad Combines Research and Creativity to Study Intersectionality of Her Heritage Weisener Lab Makes a Splash in Water Purification and Conservation More News Future Students FAQ How do I apply?
