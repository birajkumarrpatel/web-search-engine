Science at Windsor has a Faculty to Student ratio of 1:16, which is the best in Ontario and one of the best in Canada. This means that we can provide our students with unequaled transformative educational and research opportunities. The unique combination of science programs in the Faculty of Science provides our students with the ability to directly apply their passion to the most critical issues facing society now and into the future. Please let us know if you would like more information about our academic and research programs.
