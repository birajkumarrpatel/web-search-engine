A place for Undergraduate Science Collaborative and Integrative experiences. This interdisciplinary network brings together faculty and students from all Science programs to work collaboratively in providing a unique integrative support system with the goal of enriching the undergraduate student experience. Located in Room 335 in Essex Hall, the USci network is a dedicated space for experiential learning with a flexible classroom, a hands-on laboratory, and a student meeting room. We are currently working with students to develop workshops, seminars and labs in: Preparation for Academic Success in Science (PASS) Science Study Skills Exploring Career Pathways Science Meets Art (SMArt) Women in Science (WinS) Science Living Learning Community Science Alumni Hall MySci Peer Mentoring Make your connection to Science Creating a network of Science students, faculty and staff to deliver resources and provide integrative opportunities to enrich the student experience.
