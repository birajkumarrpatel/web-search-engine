For any inquiries please direct your questions to the appropriate staff member via email. Updates from the Faculty of Graduate Studies are posted on the COVID-19 webpage. Review also all important academic dates from the Office of the Registrar and the tuition refund deadlines (for students completing research requirement only). Winter term 2020 Please note some deadlines may be affected by recent events - review the Faculty of Graduate Studies updates regarding COVID-19 for details. April 3: Last day of classes REVISED: April 3, 2020 - Last day to voluntarily withdraw from Winter term courses - details. April 10 - Good Friday (University closed). April 7-20 - Winter 2020 Final Exams April 21 - Winter 2020 Alternate Final Exams Day Summer term 2020 May 1, 2020 - Format checking deadline for Spring 2020 graduation: Last day to submit for format checking dissertations, theses and major papers to the Graduate Studies office for students who are planning to meet the Spring 2020 graduation deposit deadline (May 8th) in order to graduate in Spring 2020. May 4, 2020 - Summer term classes begin for Education graduate programs May 4, 2020 - Final oral defense deadline for Spring Graduation 2020: Last day for the final oral defense of dissertations, theses and major papers for Spring Graduation 2020. May 8th - Spring Graduation 2020 deposit deadline: Last day for the final submission (deposit) for Spring Graduation 2020. Student who complete the deposit by the May 8th convocation deadline will both graduate in Spring 2020 and receive the Phase I tuition refund for the Summer term 2020 (provided they have applied to graduate and that they meet the convocation and tuition refund eligibility criteria). Students who miss the May 8th graduation deadline will not graduate in Spring 2020 but will receive the Phase I tuition refund for the Summer term 2020, provided their deposit is completed by the Phase I deadline of May 22 and they meet the tuition refund eligibility criteria. May 14 - Format checking deadline for Phase I: Last day to submit for format checking to the Graduate Studies office, for students who are planning to meet Phase I. Students should allow at least 2 business days to receive formatting feedback. May 15 - Summer term fees are due and payable. REVISED: May 19th - Summer term classes begin (all programs except Education) May 22, 2020 - Phase I deposit deadline: Last day to deposit dissertations, theses and major papers under Phase I (100% tuition refund) of the tuition fee refund policy for the Summer term 2020. May 18, 2020: Victoria Day (statutory holiday) (no classes, University closed). POSTPONED: May 26-29, 2020 - Spring 2020 Convocation ceremony REVISED: June 1 - Academic Add/Drop Date for Summer term: Last day for late registration and change of courses June 8, 2020 Format checking deadline for Phase II: last day to submit for format checking dissertations, theses and major papers to the Graduate Studies office, for students who are planning to meet the Phase II (50% tuition refund) of the tuition fee refund policy for Summer term 2020. June 15, 2020: Phase II deadline: last day for completion of all requirements for graduate degrees under Phase II (50% tuition refund) of the tuition fee refund policy for Summer term 2020. July 1, 2020 Canada Day, University closed July 28, 2020 Deadline for recommending a PhD external examiner for students who plan to graduate at the Fall Convocation August 1, 2020 No-Fee Deadline to Apply to Graduate at Fall convocation 2020. STUDENTS WHO APPLIED FOR SPRING 2020 CONVOCATION BUT DID NOT GRADUATE MUST REAPPLY FOR FALL 2020 CONVOCATION August 3, 2020 Civic Holiday (statutory holiday).
