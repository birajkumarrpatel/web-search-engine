As Ontario’s only weekend engineering management degree, the two-year, interdisciplinary program offered by the Faculty of Engineering and the Odette School of Business allows working professionals to earn their master’s degree without interrupting their careers. MEM graduates are ready to occupy leadership roles in multinational engineering and technical enterprises and contribute to manufacturing strategies, corporate innovation and operations management. Courses offered Friday evenings & Saturdays. Students acquire core competencies in applied finance, administration, market research, engineering design and product management. A two-term capstone project gives students the opportunity to work with their employer or local industry partners under the supervision of seasoned UWindsor faculty members, entrepreneurs and professional engineers. Practical Specialization Areas Manufacturing Strategy Operations Management Supply Chain Management Entrepreneurship and Innovation Project Management Program Specific Admission Requirements Deadline for Fall: Visit the MEM program webpage for information.
