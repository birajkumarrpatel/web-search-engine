right: professors Shanthi Senthe, Muharem Kianieff, Jasminka Kalajdzic, Pascale Chapdelaine This fall, Windsor Law faculty members were busy organizing significant conferences across the University of Windsor campus and community. On October 18 and 19, professors Shanthi Senthe and Muharem Kianieff co-convened the fourth annual Commercial Law Symposium at the Windsor Art Gallery in downtown Windsor. Read the full story on the Daily News. Professor Jasminka Kalajdzic organized the annual Canadian Association for Legal Ethics Conference which took place on October 25 and 26 at the University of Windsor's Windsor Hall building. Read the full story on the Daily News. On October 25, Professor Pascale Chapdelaine co-organized the Media and Space Symposium in partnership with the University of Windsor's School of Creative Arts (SOCA) and the Department of Communication, Media and Film. The symposium took place at SOCA's Alan Wildeman Centre for Creative Arts. Read the full story on the Daily News.
