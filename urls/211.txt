VIEW is the award-winning University of Windsor Alumni magazine. UWindsor is one of the most dynamic universities in Canada. It’s home to a medical education building, the world-class Ed Lumley Centre for Engineering Innovation, the Joyce Entrepreneurship Centre, an emerging downtown campus, life-changing research, and a passion for excellence. VIEW tells the stories of how UWindsor enables people to make a better world through education, scholarship, research, and engagement in a professionally written and designed publication that can be proudly shared by our grads and our community. Our story and successes are inextricably linked with those of this city and region. Distribution VIEW is mailed twice a year directly to 32,500 of the best-educated and highest-earning Windsor and Essex County readers of any local publication VIEW is mailed twice a year directly to 30,000 alumni in the rest of Ontario, including the GTA VIEW is sent directly to an impressive list of professionals, senior administrators, businesses, media, libraries, schools and hospitals, and government offices VIEW is more cost-effective than any local print publication in reaching this market Viewers Who reads VIEW?
