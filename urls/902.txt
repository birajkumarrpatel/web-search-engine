A 2-minute video competition, showcasing an idea (product or service) to solve a problem in mobility What is Mobility? Mobility refers to the ability to move or be moved freely and easily Mobility applies in many contexts, including, but not limited to: Physical mobility – ability of humans to move around their environment (i.e. physical disability and/or lack of mobility because of age) Active mobility – form of transport of people (i.e. walking and/ or cycling) Information technology mobility – ability to access information from anywhere Eligibility Must be a current University of Windsor or St. Clair College student in *any discipline* Teams must consist of at least 2 different disciplines Teams of 2-4 students Must attend Blue Sky Video Judging Night on Wednesday, March 18th, 2020 if accepted as a finalist What do I submit?
