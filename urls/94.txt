Changes to services are as follows: Please call the office for any concerns or to book a telephone appointment. Call: 519-973-7002 You will be assigned a telephone appointment with your primary care provider, and then the need for an in-office appointment will be arranged, if necessary. We will continue to renew prescriptions, but we are asking all patients to defer non-essential appointments. We request that patients do not come into the office without a scheduled in-office appointment. If you are concerned about having possibly contracted COVID-19 and still having symptoms, see the below screening tools provided by the government of Ontario and the Windsor Regional Hospital COVID-19 Assessment Centre: https://www.ontario.ca/page/2019-novel-coronavirus-covid-19-self-assessment https://www.wrh.on.ca/COVID19AssessmentCentre Click here for information on how to self monitor and self isolate. We appreciate your understanding during this challenging time as we strive to continue to provide the best possible service to our campus community.
