UWinsite Student is the University of Windsor’s student information platform. All UWinsite Student Users: Starting January 17, the login screen for UWinsite Student will look different. It will be two screens. On the first screen you will enter your UWinID@uwindsor.ca (including the @uwindsor.ca part). On the second screen you will enter your UWin Account password. Applicants: Starting January 17, you will need to sync your UWin Account before logging into UWinsite Student. This one-time sync is necessary as part of the authentication changes underway at UWindsor to keep our networks secure. To complete this step, go to Sync Your UWin Account, enter your UWin ID and Password, and click the Submit button.
