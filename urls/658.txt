We are currently seeking instructors for the following term: Summer 2020: June 8, 2020 to August 17, 2020 Please make sure to read the document titled CallforIBInstructorsSummer2020.pdf and ibadjunctjobdescriptionW19.pdf found at the bottom of this web page. Interested applicants will submit applications directly to the Faculty of Education by completing an online application. Please contact ib@uwindsor.ca should you require assistance. We thank all applicants in advance for their interest in being an IB instructor for Faculty of Education, however, only successful applicants will be contacted. The deadline for receipt of application is Sunday, March 15, 2020.
