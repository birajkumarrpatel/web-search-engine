If you like theatre, there are 88 plays on campus each year. Ticket prices for students are free or low-cost. Paintings, sculpture, bio art, film, music, theatre, literary readings — we have it all. Explore UWindsor's Campus Visit the Windsor Armouries School of Creative Arts The School of Creative Arts - or SoCA - is located in the century-old site of the Windsor Armouries and is part of our downtown campus. SoCA brings together Music, Visual Arts, and Film Production programs. Windsor Armouries houses 12 practice rooms for musicians, a performance and practice hall, a library, classrooms, offices, a keyboard and computer lab, photography and painting studios, and the Visual Arts and the Built Environment studio.
