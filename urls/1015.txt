The office of Career Development & Experiential Learning brings you the opportunity to have a real impact on the community and to gain experience and professional development, all while completing a degree program. VIP-CSL participants work with local community partners to have an impact on their community while gaining skills and experience for their future careers. Service learners will apply discipline specific knowledge while addressing community need.
