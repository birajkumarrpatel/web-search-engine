Updates on our operation will be posted on our website and social media. We will also communicate any update on the situation via email. Any question can be directed to makerspace@uwindsor.ca. Thank you for your understanding and cooperation. We will get through this together. Our makerspace, Windsor Mold Group EPIC Makers’ Base is equipped with 3D printers, CO2 laser cutter/engraver, sewing machine, and other tools for members to tinker, to learn new skills, to test new ideas, as well as to create prototypes. Membership is free for students and recent graduates from University of Windsor and St. Clair College. Check out the Make It & Take It workshop series held in the makerspace here. Location: Room 1133, Ed Lumley Centre for Engineering Innovation (CEI) The hours of operation for the makerspace vary each semester. Check back here or follow us on Facebook page for updates regarding the space. To apply for the membership, complete the application and drop off at the makerspace or email to makerspace@uwindsor.ca. You can also pick up the application package at the makerspace. Click here?to read the member handbook. Contact epicentre@uwindsor.ca for further information.
