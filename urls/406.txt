The Prize must be used for first semester tuition in the 2020/2021 academic year. The Prize covers 2020 full-time fall domestic undergraduate University of Windsor tuition for one semester minus ancillary fees. If registered as a part-time student, the Prize only covers tuition costs. The Prize is non-refundable and non-transferrable and cannot be deferred to another year. Contest entrants will receive one entry to win the Prize for completing their online profile at http://learn.uwindsor.ca/ouf19 Contest entrants agree that the University of Windsor shall be permitted to publish the name of the Prize winner. Entries will be accepted until October 5, 2019. The winner will be randomly drawn from the pool of all entries received from attendees of the Ontario Universities’ Fair who completed their online profile on or before October 5, 2019. The draws will take place on October 8, 2019 electronically at the University of Windsor. The winner will receive email notification of the award to the email address they provide when completing their profile. The award must be claimed by June 30, 2020. Participants must answer a skill testing question to receive their prize. By participating in this contest, all entrants agree to be bound by these Rules & Restrictions and agree that the University of Windsor may terminate the contest at any time without prior notice or change the Rules & Restrictions applying thereto. Contest excludes Law and Consecutive Education students. This contest is open to Ontario residents only and is governed by Canadian and Ontario law.
