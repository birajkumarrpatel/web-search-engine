This means that you will have plenty of opportunities to make new friends and participate in a variety of student clubs and events to have a memorable time at the University of Windsor! We want you to make the most of your time here and we believe that your learning experience extends far beyond the classroom. At the University of Windsor, there are countless opportunity to get involved in a variety of clubs and organizations, sports teams, support to help you along the way, and much more. University is a time for you to develop yourself academically and personally. This is the prime time to focus on yourself and your development so make sure that you find a balance at the University of Windsor that allows you to become the best version of you!
