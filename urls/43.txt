UWill Discover 2020 UPDATE The 2020 UWill Discover! Conference is CANCELLED due to the COVID-19 pandemic. In order to ensure the health and safety of everyone involved in the conference, the decision has been made to cancel the conference. We appreciate your understanding at this time. UWill Discover 2020 will be a 3-day event in which undergraduate and graduate students at the University of Windsor can present their research, scholarship, and creative work to their fellow students, friends and family, and the world!
