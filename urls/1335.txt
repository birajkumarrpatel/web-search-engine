(Formally Lynda.com) All faculty and staff can access training materials on lynda.com at no charge. How to reserve UCC Classroom? At the University Computer Centre, there is a computing classroom facility which is available for use by faculty and staff for training purposes. Seating capacity allows for a maximum of 23 users due to fire regulations. The classroom has 17 Windows 10 PCs (including the instructor’s PC), with 19” flat panel LCD monitors, various general productivity and other licensed software and an overhead projector.
