Student Third Year and Graduation Recitals Third year and graduating Music students will perform solo recitals in the Armouries Performance Hall or at a variety of venues around Windsor. This spring, these recitals will be performed without a live audience due to public health concerns during the COVID-19 outbreak. Community Concert series at SoCA Armouries. Sundays at 2 pm these free recitals feature performances by young musicians in the studios of ORMTA teachers and other music teachers in our community. Purchase your tickets to any of our concerts in advance online at our secure website or buy at the door. Our goal is to inform and connect music lovers, whether they are students, alumni, or our friends in the community.
