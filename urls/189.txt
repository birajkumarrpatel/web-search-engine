Driving directions to main campus From Hwy. 401: Exit Hwy. 401 at Hwy 3 West (Huron Church Road), exit number 5. Take Huron Church Road, around the Ambassador Bridge Entrance, to Wyandotte St. W. Turn right (east) on Wyandotte Street West and right (south) on Sunset Avenue. The parking structure is on left side of the street. From Windsor Airport: From the main airport parking lot, turn right (west) onto County Road 42. County Road 42 turns into Division Road at Walker Road. Continue along Division Road approximately 1 kilometer; the road forks. Take the left fork to Cabana Road. Continue along Cabana for approximately 4.5 kilometers to Huron Church Road. Turn right (north) onto Huron Church Road to Patricia Road, approximately 6.25 kilometers. Stay to right and proceed past bridge entrance along Patricia Rd. Turn right (east) onto Wyandotte Street, right (south) onto Sunset Ave, to the parking structure. From VIA Rail station: Proceed west along Riverside Drive approximately 5 kilometers to Sunset Ave. Turn left (south) on California Ave. Turn right (west) on Wyandotte Street West and left (south) on Sunset Avenue. The parking structure is on the left side of the street. From Ambassador Bridge: Once you have cleared Canada Customs, take the bridge exit on the extreme right that indicates downtown Windsor. This will bring you to Huron Church Road, traveling north. Turn right (east) onto Wyandotte Street, right (south) onto Sunset Ave, to the parking structure. From Windsor-Detroit Tunnel: Once you have cleared Canada Customs, turn left (west) onto Park Street. Immediately turn left (south) onto Ouellette Avenue. Proceed to Wyandotte Street West. Turn right (west) onto Wyandotte Street West, approximately 2 kilometers to Sunset Ave. Left (south) onto Sunset Ave, to the parking structure. Driving directions to south campus From Hwy. 401: Exit Hwy. 401 at Hwy 3 West (Huron Church Road), exit number 5. Take Huron Church Road to College Avenue. Turn right (east) on to College Avenue to parking lots G or H for the Faculty of Human Kinetics, South Campus Stadium and St. Denis Centre. For the School of Visual Arts (LeBel Building), turn left (west) on to College Avenue. Turn left (south) into parking lot P. From Windsor Airport: From the main airport parking lot, turn right (west) onto County Road 42. County Road 42 turns into Division Road at Walker Road. Continue along Division Road approximately 1 kilometer; the road forks. Take the left fork to Cabana Road. Continue along Cabana for approximately 4.5 kilometers to Huron Church Road. Turn right (north) onto Huron Church Road, approximately 6.25 kilometers to College Avenue. To School of Visual Arts (LeBel Building): Turn left (west) onto College Avenue to parking lot P. To Faculty of Human Kinetics, South Campus Stadium, St. Denis Athletics Centre: Turn right (east) onto College Avenue to parking lots G or H. From VIA Rail station: Proceed west along Riverside Drive approximately 5 kilometers to Huron Church Road. Left (south) onto Huron Church Road to College Avenue. To School of Visual Arts (LeBel Building): Turn right (west) onto College Avenue to parking lot P. To Faculty of Human Kinetics, South Campus Stadium, St. Denis Athletics Centre: Turn left (east) onto College Avenue to parking lots G or H. From Ambassador Bridge: Once you have cleared Canada Customs, take the main bridge exit on the left to College Avenue. To School of Visual Arts (LeBel Building): Turn right (west) onto College Avenue to parking lot P. To Faculty of Human Kinetics, South Campus Stadium, St. Denis Athletics Centre: Turn left (east) onto College Avenue to parking lots G or H. From Windsor-Detroit Tunnel: Once you have cleared Canada Customs, turn left (west) onto Park Street. Immediately turn right (north) onto Ouellette Avenue. Proceed to University Avenue, the next traffic light. Turn left (west) onto University Ave, approximately 2.25 kilometers to Huron Church Road. Left (south) onto Huron Church Road to College Avenue. To School of Visual Arts (LeBel Building): Turn right (west) onto College Avenue to parking lot P. To Faculty of Human Kinetics, South Campus Stadium, St. Denis Athletics Centre: Turn left (east) onto College Avenue to parking lots G or H. Driving directions to downtown campuses To Windsor Hall: Head east on Riverside Drive, approximately 2 kilometres to Ferry Street, right on Ferry, one block south to 167 Ferry St. For Windsor Hall, find more options for routes and means of transportation. To School of Creative Arts: Head east on University Avenue, approximately 2.5 kilometres to Freedom Way. For The Alan Wildeman School of Creative Arts, find more options for routes and means of transportation. Public Transportation The University of Windsor is served by Transit Windsor, which operates buses from 5 a.m. to 2 a.m. weekdays. Four Transit Windsor bus routes bring passengers to the University of Windsor.
